The Video Room

The Hoppy PalmTree provides 3 high-def televisions for your sports viewing pleasure.
In the spring and summer, golf, NASCAR® racing and MLB Extra Innings by DIRECTV® will be offered exclusively.
When the blustery winds of fall and winter arrive, so does college and NFL football!
NFL Sunday Ticket by DIRECTV® brings you every out-of-market game in the NFL!
And, if you are a Dallas Cowboys fan, this is the place for you. The Cowboys are sure to be televised here!
As a note, the Hoppy PalmTree may not be family friendly during Dallas games!
